## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It is used by companies to manage customer details, sales, support and communication in one place.

---

## What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage customers, sales, services and business processes.

---

## Why companies use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it helps them:
- manage customer data
- track sales
- automate tasks
- improve communication
- understand customers better

---

## Salesforce Concepts

### Account
An account represents a company or organization.

Example:
- Amazon
- Infosys
- TCS

### Contact
A contact represents a person related to an account.

Example:
- customer
- employee
- student

### Opportunity
An opportunity represents a possible business deal or sale.

---

## Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

### Explanation
- Lead = interested person
- Contact = confirmed person details
- Opportunity = possible deal
- Customer = final customer

## Difference Between Admin and Developer

### Admin
- manages Salesforce setup
- creates reports and flows
- works mostly without coding

### Developer
- writes Apex code
- builds applications
- adds custom functionality
